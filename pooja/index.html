Welcome to Heart Health Prediction

The purpose of this project is to provide a prediction mechanism for heart health levels.

The website provides a page to provide inputs to predict your heart health. Alternatively you can look at the analysis done on a user set and see the trend.





The analysis is done on the below factors:

  • slope_of_peak_exercise_st_segment (type: int): the slope of the peak exercise ST segment, an electrocardiography read out indicating quality of blood flow to the heart
  • thal (type: categorical): results of thallium stress test measuring blood flow to the heart, with possible values normal, fixed_defect, reversible_defect
  • resting_blood_pressure (type: int): resting blood pressure
  • chest_pain_type (type: int): chest pain type (4 values)
  • num_major_vessels (type: int): number of major vessels (0-3) colored by flourosopy
  • fasting_blood_sugar_gt_120_mg_per_dl (type: binary): fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl
  • resting_ekg_results (type: int): resting electrocardiographic results (values 0,1,2)
  • serum_cholesterol_mg_per_dl (type: int): serum cholestoral in mg/dl
  • oldpeak_eq_st_depression (type: float): oldpeak = ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest, a measure of abnormality in electrocardiograms
  • sex (type: binary): 0: female, 1: male
  • age (type: int): age in years
  • max_heart_rate_achieved (type: int): maximum heart rate achieved (beats per minute)
  • exercise_induced_angina (type: binary): exercise-induced chest pain (0: False, 1: True)
